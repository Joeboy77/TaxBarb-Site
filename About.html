OUR HISTORY

Founded in 1995, TaxBarb Parlour began as a small
family-owned barbershop nestled in the heart of
downtown. With a passion for precision and a
commitment to excellence, our founder, Robert "Barb"
Johnson, transformed his humble establishment into a
beloved local institution. Over the years, TaxBarb
Parlour has evolved, blending traditional techniques
with modern styles, while maintaining the warm,
welcoming atmosphere that has made us a community
staple. Today, we proudly continue our legacy of
providing top-notch grooming services, ensuring every
client leaves looking and feeling their best.
